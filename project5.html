IoT Based Pet Feeder

Smart Automated Feeding System Using ESP32 and Blynk IoT

The IoT-based pet feeder is a smart automated feeding system that allows pet owners to feed their pets remotely and on schedule via a mobile app controlled by Blynk IoT platform. Below is a detailed description of the working, components used, and how the system operates:

Detailed Description

Working Principle

The pet feeder consists of a wooden stand on which a food container is fixed.

A servo motor is attached to the wooden stand, which acts as a lid opener for the food container.

Below the food container, there is a food tray where the pet can eat the dispensed food.

The servo motor rotates to open or close the lid of the food container, controlling the release of food into the tray.

The servo motor is connected to an ESP32 microcontroller that controls its operation.

The ESP32 is programmed to communicate with the Blynk IoT platform, enabling the user to remotely control the servo motor via the Blynk mobile app.

Features include scheduled feeding times, allowing users to set specific times for automatic feeding.

Portion control is maintained by controlling the servo motor’s rotation duration or angle, ensuring consistent food amounts.

Real-time notifications via the Blynk app keep the owner informed about feeding status or any malfunctions.

The overall system enhances convenience, ensuring pets are fed on time even in the owner's absence, while prioritizing the pet's well-being.

How it Works

Setup: The food container and tray are mounted on a wooden stand with the servo motor attached to the container lid mechanism.

Control: The servo motor’s signal pin is connected to a GPIO pin on the ESP32. Power and ground connections complete the circuit.

Firmware: The ESP32 is flashed with Arduino code to connect to WiFi and the Blynk cloud platform. It listens for commands from the Blynk app to operate the servo.

Feeding Operation:

  • The user remotely opens the Blynk app and either triggers feed manually or sets a timer for scheduled feeding.
  • At command, the ESP32 activates the servo motor to rotate, lifting or sliding the lid open momentarily, allowing food to drop into the tray.
  • The motor then returns the lid to the closed position to prevent spillage or contamination.

Notifications: The Blynk app receives feedback about feeding status and alerts the user if any fault occurs or feeding is complete.

Schedule and Control: Users can customize feeding schedules, portion sizes (by adjusting servo rotation time), and receive usage logs in realtime.

Benefits

  • Enables pet feeding from anywhere with internet access.
  • Scheduled automated feeding avoids missed meals.
  • Portion control prevents overfeeding.
  • Real-time notifications ensure pet is fed safely.
  • Simple mechanical setup with widely available components.
  • Scalable to multiple feeding times or integration with sensors.

This IoT pet feeder powered by ESP32 and controlled by the Blynk platform provides a reliable, convenient, and caring solution for modern pet owners to manage feeding remotely and efficiently.
